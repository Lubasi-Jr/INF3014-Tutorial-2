Who am I?

I am a student at the University of Cape Town with a deep passion for software development and book reading. My journey into the world of tech and literature led me to start an online store. After realizing how much these two interests shaped my life, I decided to create a platform that caters to avid readers, entrepreneurs, parents, and gift buyers.
